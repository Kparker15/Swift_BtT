# Controls
Keyboard  
Space Bar: Jump  
Right Arrow: Slash  
Down Arrow: Slide  

Controller  
Right Bumper: Jump  
Bottom Face Button: Slide  
Right Face Button: Slash  

Phone  
Tap the left side of the screen to jump  
Swipe down on the right side of the screen to slide  
Swipe right on the right side of the screen to slash
